A delicious pasta recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara is an italian spaghetti recipe that has a creamy sauce topped with bacon.

The sauce is made up of eggs and cheese with a bit of additional seasoning. It is an easy and delicious recipe that the whole family will love

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 garlic Cloves, minced
  • 3 large eggs plus 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Bacon:
    1. Bring bacon and water to simmer in 10 inch nonstick skillet over meium heat; cook until water evaporates and bacon begins to sizzle, about 8 minutes
    2. Reduce heat to medium-low and continue to cook until fat renders and bacon browns, 5 to 8 minutes longer
    3. Add garlic and cook, stirring constantly for about 30 seconds
    4. Strain bacon mixture through fine-mesh strainer set in bowl
    5. Set bacon aside
    6. Reserve 1 tablespoon of fat
  2. Pasta:
    1. While the bacon is cooking, bring 2 quarts water to boil in a large pot
    2. Set colander in large bowl
    3. Add spaghetti and salt to pot
    4. Cook, stiring frequently, until al dente (about 8-10 minutes)
