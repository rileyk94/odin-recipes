Baked Potato

Home

A simple but delicious meal

Easy to make with very few ingredients, baked potatos are a great staple to add to your diet

Ingredients

  • 1 medium russet potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. Scrub the potato, then pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork
  3. Rub the skin with olive oil, then sprinkle with salt
  4. Once the oven reaches temperature, place the potato in the oven and bake until slightly soft and golden brown, about 90 minutes
  5. Slice the potato down the center, add butter
  6. Once butter is melted, add black pepper and cheese
  7. Consider adding more toppings, like bacon or sour cream, but otherwise enjoy
