Creamy Ramen Noodles

Home

an easy and delicious way to try something new with your package of ramen noodles

combine ramen noodles with chicken, seasoning, and cream to make a brand new dish

Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles
  • 1 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 pinch everything bagel seasoning (optional)
Instructions
  1. Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat
  2. Add minced garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 30 seconds
  3. Add chicen broth, cream, and ramen noodles. Cook, stirring occasionally, until noodles soften, about 3 minutes
  4. Add soy sauce and chopped cooked chicen. Allow to get hot
  5. Serve garnished with everything bagel seasoning if desired
